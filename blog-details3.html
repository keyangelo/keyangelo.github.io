Ma Prairie

By Michel-ange June 10, 2021

Ma Prairie is a restaurant-bar for which I ensured a total revision of the website

it proposes a kitchen exclusively made up of fresh products for traditional dishes which will know how to conquer you by the small key of originality brought by their chief. It is within a restful framework and in a convivial environment that we will be made a pleasure of delighting your senses.

For the realization of this site, I had to ensure the referencing of each product, page, description, link knowing that they changed every two weeks. I set up a reservation tool to receive by mail the reservations via the site. For the bar part it was easier to do the same thing.

Wordpress website.

For the creation of the product pages, they were made so that the customer does not have any questions about the product and so that it is the best possible customer journey.

For this company, I also created visuals and templates for their digital communication. I also designed the menu for their restaurant.
