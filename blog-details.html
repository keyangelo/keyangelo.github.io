Jersey website

By Michel-ange Mar 15, 2020

The main objective of the E-commerce site, "Jersey", is to allow the sale of soccer shirts to fans, enthusiasts and athletes.

The Jersey website will focus on the sale of jerseys from the 5 major European leagues. Jersey will also offer, in a category called "others", some of the most popular jerseys from less popular leagues.The overall objective is to make money through the sale of official jerseys to soccer enthusiasts.

Prestashop.

For the creation of this site I took part in the creation of contents in its totality (Creation of the products, Installation of the declensions, description, title, meta title, price, reference etc...)

2 900 players

800 football jersey.

110 TEAMS and 70,000 references

Website made with Prestashop.

The jersey are for the season 2019-2020.
